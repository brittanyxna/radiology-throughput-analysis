# Radiology Throughput Analysis: CT vs MRI 🏥

## Project Overview
This project analyzes radiology throughput by comparing CT and MRI scan wait times and procedure volumes across different quarters of the year. The purpose is to identify trends that may impact imaging workflow efficiency, patient access, and operational performance.

---

## Research Question
How do CT and MRI wait times and procedure volumes compare across different quarters of the year, and what trends may affect radiology throughput and patient care?

---

## Objectives
- Compare CT and MRI procedure volumes
- Evaluate patient wait times across imaging modalities
- Identify seasonal throughput trends
- Explore workflow efficiency in radiology operations
- Visualize imaging data using Python

---
